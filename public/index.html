About me

I love what I do. There is something about technology that connects people in a way that nothing else can. It has the ability to change lives and that is something I'm very proud to be apart of. I was introduced to web development in 2016 and the rest is history. One of the things I love most about programming is the continuous learning required for this fast-paced environment.
